About Me

Hi, my name is Paul, and I live in the suburbs of Philadelphia. I grew up in this area, and then went down to Washington, D.C. for college.

I majored in Economics, which I enjoyed. But when I arrived at my first job out of college, I found myself interacting mostly with the software engineers at the company. I could tell I had developed a newfound interest.

With that feeling, I enrolled in a few Computer Science courses online. In a short period of time, I was able to land my first Web Developer position in Northern Virginia.

Currently, I mentor and instruct students at Thinkful, Inc., whose mission is to train the next generation of Software Engineers, Web Developers, Data Analysts, and Data Scientists. Having been immersed in code during my time at Thinkful, my technical skills are thankfully up to date.
